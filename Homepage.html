Site Information

  This is an website that serves as an information hub for anything that is related to the Dungeons and Dragons campaign that takes place in the World of Aulter. While specifically tailored to this campaign, most of the information present here, such as the bestiary, equipment, magical items, and spells are all accurate to the Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition rulebooks, player handbook, and monster manual. This means that while there may be information pertaining to this campaign present, the stats, descriptions, ect. are all safe to use for non-homebrew campaigns and in other works, as long as it complies with the Wizards of the Coast OGL, Fan Content Policy, Creative Commons, and SRD.
