The Capital of Inen

A sprawling port city, Inen is known for its diverse market, advance harbor, and acclaimed adventurer's guild. Being the capital of the Inenian Tetrarchy, Inen sports a large guard presence. Combined with the adventurer's guild, Inen is considered a safe place to work and live.
